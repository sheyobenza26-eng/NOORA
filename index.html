Our Story

I searched for a hijab that was gentle on my sensitive scalp, breathable enough for all-day wear, and elegant enough to feel confident in. When I couldn't find one, I decided to create it. NOORA was born from a simple belief: Every woman deserves comfort without sacrificing confidence.
