Our Story

I searched for a hijab that was gentle on my sensitive scalp, breathable enough for all-day wear, and elegant enough to feel confident in. Every fabric I tried was either too warm, too heavy, or uncomfortable after only a few hours. When I couldn't find one, I decided to create it.

NOORA was born from the belief that every woman deserves comfort, confidence, and timeless elegance.
